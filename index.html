About Me

I am a recent graduate from Yale University with a B.A. in History and a certificate in Data Science. My history background compels me to understand how the past shapes today's challenges. I'm eager to apply my historical narrative and storytelling skills to sports media, uncovering the compelling human stories that make athletics so powerful. As a lifelong sports fan (go Bears!), I'm particularly interested in sports journalism, where I can blend my analytical background with my passion for athletics to explore the intersection of sports, society, and social justice. I'm excited to bring fresh perspectives to athletic culture and crafting narratives that reflect our broader cultural moment.
